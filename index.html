Our Story Since 1952

~ ⚜ ~

Founded by Arthur & Margaret Pembroke, The Antique Emporium has been serving discerning collectors for over seven decades.

Our family believes that furniture carries the soul of its era. Each scratch, each patina, each worn edge tells a story of life lived beautifully. We travel extensively to source authentic pieces, ensuring every item meets our exacting standards for provenance and condition.

"We don't merely sell furniture—we preserve history."

The Pembroke Family
